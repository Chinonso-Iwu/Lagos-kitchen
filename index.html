Jollof Rice & Grilled Chicken

A popular West African dish made with rice simmered in a rich tomato-based sauce, often served with spicy, juicy grilled chicken. It's a party favorite across Nigeria and beyond.

Ingredients
  • Long grain parboiled rice
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Red bell peppers
  • Scotch bonnet (ata rodo)
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Vegetable oil
  • Chicken or beef stock
  • Thyme
  • Bay leaves
  • Curry powder
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Chicken parts (drumsticks, thighs, etc.)
  • Onion powder
  • Thyme
  • Chili powder or cayenne
  • Lemon juice or vinegar
